HOW IT WORKS NOW (The Complete Flow)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1: ADMIN CREATES CAMPAIGN
   Location: /campaign-portal?access=ferrero-admin-2025
   Action: Fills form and clicks "Send Campaign"
   Result: Campaign inserted into offer_campaigns table

STEP 2: SUPABASE PUBLISHES REAL-TIME EVENT
   Table: offer_campaigns
   Event: INSERT (new row added)
   Targets: All connected browsers via WebSocket

STEP 3: RETAILER'S BROWSER RECEIVES EVENT
   Subscription: AppContext.jsx real-time listener
   Check: Is campaign targeted to this user's role? (retailer/distributor)
   Result: YES → Proceed to Step 4

STEP 4: TOAST NOTIFICATION TRIGGERED
   Function: showToast()
   Action: Dispatches CustomEvent('show-toast')
   Result: Toast appears on screen

STEP 5: CAMPAIGN APPEARS IN APP
   Reload: loadActiveCampaigns() called
   Display: Campaign added to "Active Offers" section
   Notification: Campaign added to Notifications screen
